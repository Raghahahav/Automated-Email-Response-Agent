2. INTRODUCTION TO THE REFERRAL PROGRAM
2.1 Purpose of the Referral Program
The referral program has been introduced to:
* Encourage existing clients to refer new users
* Reward both the referrer and the referee
* Expand the client base organically
* Maintain compliance with all regulatory requirements
The program is designed to be simple, transparent, and incentive-driven, without misleading claims or guaranteed returns.
________________


2.2 Launch Timeline
* Referral program launch date: 1 January 2026
* Program is ongoing unless officially discontinued or modified
* Any changes to the program will be communicated via:
   * Email
   * Official website
   * In-app notifications
________________


3. REFERRAL PROGRAM STRUCTURE
3.1 Eligibility Criteria
Referrer Eligibility
A referrer must:
* Be an existing registered client of the company
* Have a verified trading account
* Be KYC-compliant
* Not have any account suspension or regulatory restrictions
Referee Eligibility
A referee must:
* Be a new user (no prior account with the company)
* Complete full KYC verification
* Open a trading account using the referral link or referral code
* Not be an immediate family member of an employee (as per compliance rules)
________________


3.2 Referral Process
1. Existing client generates a referral link or code
2. Referee signs up using the referral link/code
3. Referee completes:
   * Account opening
   * KYC verification
4. Referee performs the first qualifying trade
5. Referral reward is credited after validation
________________


3.3 Qualifying Action
A referral is considered successful only when:
* The referee completes KYC successfully
* The referee executes at least one trade (buy or sell)
* The trade value is ₹500 or more
Merely signing up without trading does not qualify for referral benefits.

5. LIMITATIONS & RESTRICTIONS
5.1 Referral Limits
* Maximum referrals per client: 20 per financial year
* Excess referrals beyond the limit will not earn rewards
* The company reserves the right to revise referral limits
________________


5.2 Prohibited Activities
The following actions are strictly prohibited:
* Self-referrals
* Creating multiple accounts
* Fake or fraudulent referrals
* Manipulating trades solely to gain rewards
* Public promotion in misleading or non-compliant ways
Violation may result in:
* Reward cancellation
* Account suspension
* Permanent termination in severe cases
________________
